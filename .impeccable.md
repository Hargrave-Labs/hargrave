## Design Context

### Users
Technically literate young professionals at SMBs. They're evaluating whether Hargrave Labs can build something impressive for their business. They arrive skeptical and need to leave thinking "these people are the real deal."

### Brand Personality
Impressive. Professional. Aspirational.

The site itself IS the portfolio — visitors should want their own site to look and feel like this one. Every detail signals craft and competence.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Premium studio aesthetic with dark/light section contrast
- "Floating card" architecture with rounded dark sections on white page
- Emerald green accent (#34D399) as the signature color
- Grain texture for tactile depth
- Glass/frosted effects on dark surfaces
- Animation-rich but purposeful — GSAP for scroll, Framer Motion for interaction
- Dark theme for immersive sections, light for content/trust sections

### Design Principles
1. **The site is the proof** — every interaction should demonstrate the quality of work Hargrave Labs delivers
2. **Impressive, not flashy** — sophistication over spectacle, precision over excess
3. **Technically credible** — the audience knows good code when they see it; the site should feel engineered, not templated
4. **Every pixel earns its place** — flagship quality, no shortcuts, no generic patterns
